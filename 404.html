Harsh L Shah
PhD Research Scholar
Civil Engineering Department
IIT Gandhinagar

Email: harsh.lovekumar.shah@iitgn.ac.in

Google Scholar , Research Gate Profile


Awards

  1. Ph.D. in Water Resources Engineering, IIT Gandhinagar (2013-Present)
  2. Bachelor of Technology in Civil Engineering, L.D. (2009-2013)
Research Interests
  1. Hydrologic modeling
  2. Influence of anthropogenic activities (e.g., Irrigation and Reservoirs) on water budget
  3. Climate change impacts on water resources
Achievements
  1. Alternative Sponsored Fellow (ASF) at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL), USA (January-June, 2018).
  2. The Inter-Sectoral Impact Model Intercomparison Project (ISIMIP) - Travel grant to attend the ISIMIP workshop and Impacts World Conference (2017).
  3. Ministry of Human Resources Fellowship; IIT Gandhinagar Fellowship (2013-2019).
  4. The Gujarat Institute of Civil Engineers & Architects gold medal - For securing 1st rank in 3rd and 4th year of B.Tech civil engineering (2012-2013).
